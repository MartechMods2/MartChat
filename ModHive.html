Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install mods on my device?
Installation varies by platform. For Android, enable unknown sources and use a file manager. For PC, follow mod-specific instructions. For emulators like PPSSPP, place mod files in the appropriate folder. Each mod includes detailed installation guides.
Are all mods safe and tested?
Yes! All mods on ModHive are scanned for malware, tested for stability, and verified by our professional team. We maintain the highest security standards to protect our community. Every mod includes safety badges and testing details.
Can I create and sell my own mods?
Absolutely! ModHive supports creators. You can upload your mods, set pricing, and earn revenue directly. We handle payments securely and provide analytics to track your mod performance and sales.
What platforms do you support?
We support PC (Windows, Mac, Linux), Android, iOS, and popular emulators like PPSSPP, Dolphin, Citra, and more. Our mod database covers all major gaming platforms with platform-specific optimizations.
Is customer support really 24/7?
Yes! Our global support team is available 24/7 via email, WhatsApp, Telegram, and our help center. Average response time is under 2 hours. We also have a thriving community forum for peer support.
How do I use Martbot for mod search?
Martbot is our intelligent mod finder bot available on Telegram and WhatsApp. Simply message it with your game name or mod preferences, and it instantly searches and recommends mods. It learns from your preferences for personalized recommendations.

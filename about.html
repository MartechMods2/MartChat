About ModHive

ModHive is a hub for gamers and modders — a place to discover, share and improve game experiences across PC, mobile and emulators. Founded by MarTech, ModHive focuses on technology, creative modding and exploring the game world with a community-first approach.

Our Mission Accessible Mods

Make high-quality mods and practical game knowledge accessible to every player and creator, while keeping security, compatibility and performance at the center of everything we publish.

The Story

What started as a small project to collect useful tweaks and guides grew into a full platform where creators can submit mods, players can find trusted content, and teams can collaborate on larger mod packs. We grew by listening to our community and shipping practical tools.

Meet the Founder

Martins — CEO

Martins is the CEO and heart of ModHive. A lifelong technophile, he fell in love with the technology and gaming worlds early on and has built a varied skillset across development, community building and mod creation. He leads ModHive with a focus on quality, curiosity and helping creators thrive.

FAQ About ModHive

When was ModHive founded?

ModHive was founded in 2024 by MarTech with a mission to make premium game mods accessible to all gamers. The platform grew rapidly through community feedback and creator support.

How many creators are on ModHive?

We have thousands of verified creators contributing custom mods, walkthroughs and gaming guides. Our quality assurance team reviews every submission.

Is ModHive open source?

While ModHive maintains proprietary security standards, we're committed to transparency. Creator tools and templates are available for the community.

How do I contact ModHive?

Reach us via email at ibrahimmartinex@gmail.com, WhatsApp, Telegram, or use the contact form at contact.html. We respond within 2 hours.

What is Martbot?

Martbot is our AI-powered mod search assistant available on Telegram and WhatsApp. Simply message your game or mod preferences for instant personalized recommendations.

What platforms does ModHive support?

We support PC (Windows, Mac, Linux), Android, iOS, and emulators including PPSSPP, Dolphin, Citra and more. Each platform has optimized mod collections.
